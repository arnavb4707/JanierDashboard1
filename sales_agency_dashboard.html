Google Sheet Connection

This dashboard reads your sheet live (no Apps Script deployment needed) — it just needs the spreadsheet ID and the tab (gid) for each role. Find a tab's gid in its URL after #gid= when that tab is open.

Adding the Phone Setter / Closer Call Log tabs

Currently only the DM Setter Input tab exists, so Reps/Call Log show that role only. To light up the rest, add new tabs to the same spreadsheet with these columns (order matters), then paste their gid above:

Phone Setter Input tab columns: Name | Date | Dials | Connections | Qualified Convos | Sets | Calls On Calendar | Live Calls | Closes | Downsells | Cash Collected | Total Revenue | Rate Today 1-10 | Objections | Notes Closer Call Log tab columns: Closer | Date | Email | Summary | Offer | Status | Cash Collected | Total Revenue | Recording Link

Status values the dashboard understands: Closed, Deposit, Follow Up, No Show, Lost, Bad Fit, DQ, IGNORE, Cancelled.
